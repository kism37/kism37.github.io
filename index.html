Who I Am

I'm a security engineer with an offensive security focus, currently working at Enovise while completing my NYSC year in Nigeria. I hold a CompTIA Pentest+ certification and am actively working toward OSCP.

My work sits at the intersection of breaking things and building them — from vulnerability research and bug bounty hunting to developing tools that solve real-world security problems. I have a particular interest in financial systems, web app security, and automation.

When I'm not hunting bugs I build: an AI-powered NFL prediction system targeting data-driven sports analysis, and an intelligence scanner for detecting high-risk investment schemes. I like problems that require both technical depth and systems thinking.
